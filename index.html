About Us

It's been a long journey for Golden Chicken Pvt. Ltd. to be what it is today. It goes back to the year 1991, when the company was founded by Rahul Shetty. The Company started as a Feed Manufacturing firm with a first year turnover of Rs. 1.2 crores. It diversified with forward integration into contract broiler farming.. It has since grown to have its operations in Maharashtra

At Golden Chicken, We are big meat-lovers. And by big, We mean huge. So when it Comes to the meat we put on your plate , we're extremely picky . Every single Product is handpicked by a team with years of experience.

We Will Sell only the meat that we would eat Ourselves.

Our chickens always weigh between 1 and 1.5 kg because we've found that bigger birds tend to be rubbery and fibrous

If it's not fresh, We won't sell it.

Thanks for Reading. Now you know what we've set out to do and how we've set out to do and how we're doing it. if you find us slipping, haul us up, We'll set it right and ensure that all is happy again. That's the golden chicken Promise.

Bon appetit!
